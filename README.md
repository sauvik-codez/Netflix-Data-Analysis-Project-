# 📺 Netflix Data Analysis

This project explores and analyzes the **Netflix Movies and TV Shows dataset** to uncover insights about content distribution, trends, and patterns across genres, release years, and countries. The analysis is performed in **Python** using libraries like Pandas, NumPy, Matplotlib, and Seaborn.
For Dashboard view this repo:[Dashboard](https://github.com/sauvik-codez/NETFLIX-DASHBOARD)
---

## 🧠 Project Overview

The goal of this notebook is to perform **exploratory data analysis (EDA)** on Netflix’s catalog to understand:

* The type and volume of content available on the platform
* How movies and TV shows are distributed by country, genre, and release year
* Popular content categories and patterns over time
* Data-driven insights that could help understand Netflix’s content strategy

---

## 📂 Dataset

* **Name:** Netflix Movies and TV Shows Dataset
* **Source:** [Kaggle – Netflix Titles Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/shivamb/netflix-shows)
* **Content:**

  * `show_id`, `type`, `title`, `director`, `cast`, `country`, `date_added`, `release_year`, `rating`, `duration`, `listed_in`, `description`
* The dataset contains both **movies and TV shows** available on Netflix as of the extraction date.

---

## ⚙️ Technologies Used

* **Python 3**
* **Libraries:**

  * `pandas` – Data manipulation and cleaning
  * `numpy` – Numerical operations
  * `matplotlib` & `seaborn` – Data visualization
  * `plotly` *(optional)* – Interactive charts
* **Power-BI**
---

## 🚀 Steps Performed

1. **Data Loading and Cleaning**

   * Handled missing values and duplicates
   * Standardized date formats and text data

2. **Exploratory Data Analysis**

   * Distribution of content types (Movies vs. TV Shows)
   * Most common genres and countries
   * Trend of content releases over the years
   * Ratings and duration patterns

3. **Visualization**

   * Bar plots, heatmaps, and trend lines for deeper insights
   * Correlation and frequency analysis

4. *Dashboard creation*

---

## 📊 Key Insights

* Movies dominate the Netflix catalog compared to TV shows.
* The US and India contribute the highest number of titles.
* A large portion of content was added between 2015–2020.
* Drama and International content are among the most frequent genres.
